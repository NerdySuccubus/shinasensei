VIDEOS


I specialize in the creation of multimedia content for different platforms, mostly TikTok & Youtube. Teaching Japanese is only one of my many hobbies and hustles!

I love learning languages, so I figured why not share what I’ve learned after some good years with this language and after living in Japan for three years.

I’ve found that learning through videos is the most dynamic way to study, so most of the content will be in that format. I also offer even more content in my courses and on my Patreon Page!



ARTICLES


I have a monthly newsletter available for my Patreon supporters where I tell stories, a mix of my experiences while living in Japan and travelling a bit of the world and some advice of what I’ve taken from those experiences.

The Mini-books are little, but resourceful, compilations of many topics that will help you learn the language and many aspects of Japanese culture (Vocabulary, grammar, study tips & more).

I really believe they can add a lot of value to your learning experience together with all the video content I have available for free on many platforms.
